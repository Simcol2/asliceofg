The Origin

Not just a cake.
A tradition.

Every rum cake carries a story — this one started in a kitchen with a recipe that refused to stay ordinary.

A Slice of G was born from a love of bold flavour and the belief that good food should make people stop mid-conversation. Every cake is handcrafted in small batches, soaked to perfection, and made entirely from scratch — no shortcuts, no compromises.

Based in Toronto, we serve everything from individual treats to full event catering. If you've ever had a rum cake you couldn't stop thinking about, you already know what we're about.
